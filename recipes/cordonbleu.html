Cordon Bleu

Description

Chicken Cordon Bleu is a dish composed of chicken breasts, ham, and Swiss cheese

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces boneless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 12 slices thin sliced ham
  • 1 piece raw egg
  • 2 tsp paprika
