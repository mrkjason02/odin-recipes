Sinigang

Description

Sinigang na baboy is a filipino pork soup cooked in a sour broth.

This is one of my comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo of pork meat
  • Tomato
  • Kangkong
  • Okra
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Pour water into a cooking pot. Let boil
  2. Add onion and tomato. Boil for 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. Put the pork in the cooking pot. Boil for 25 minutes.
  4. Not Yet Done!
