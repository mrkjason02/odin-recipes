Philly Cheese Steak

Description

Philly cheesesteak is made with sirloin sliced into strips and seasoned with a delicious blend of herbs and spices for a flavorsome sandwich.

This is one of my favorite food.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 3 ounces Swiss cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into thin 2 inch strips
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Not Yet Done!

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix salt, pepper, paprika, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, marjoram, and basil together in a small bowl.
  3. Place steak in a large bowl
